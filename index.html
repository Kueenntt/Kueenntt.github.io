About Groove

Welcome to Groove, where culinary excellence meets musical brilliance. As part of my Aesthetics course in the Bhutan Baccalaureate program, I've combined my passion for music theory and performance with gourmet dining to create this unique concept.

At Groove, we believe that music and food share a common language of rhythm, harmony, and expression. Our chefs compose dishes that harmonize with the musical performances, creating a multisensory experience that delights both the palate and the ears.

Drawing from my studies in music theory and practical experience with guitar and drums, I've curated a space where every element works in concert to create an unforgettable atmosphere.

Music Theory in Practice

At Groove, we apply fundamental music theory concepts to create harmonious dining experiences

Harmony

Musical Instruments

Showcasing the instruments I've mastered through my Aesthetics studies

Guitar

From classical techniques to contemporary styles, the guitar offers versatile expression across genres. I've studied chord progressions, fingerstyle patterns, and improvisation techniques.

Drums

As the foundation of rhythm, drums create the heartbeat of music. I've practiced various time signatures, polyrhythms, and dynamic control to build solid rhythmic foundations.

Piano

The perfect instrument for understanding music theory, harmony, and composition. I've used piano to study chord structures and melodic development.
